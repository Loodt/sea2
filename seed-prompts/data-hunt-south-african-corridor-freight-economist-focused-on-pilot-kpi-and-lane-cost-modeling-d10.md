## Key Decisions
The unilateral one-carrier pilot case is viable if it is modeled as corridor-level cost avoidance and utilization improvement rather than as rate uplift or marketplace network effects. JHB-CPT is the best first lane because public sources directly expose route distance, road share, cost shares, toll-plaza tariffs, and a current diesel-cost anchor. The minimum viable KPI stack is empty-km percentage, loaded-km share, turnaround or reload interval, trips per truck per month, toll-plus-fuel cost per trip, and gross contribution per trip, with admin or cash-timing modeled separately. The resulting business case should be expressed as a bounded sensitivity model until one carrier supplies baseline empty-running, payload, utilization, and if relevant POD-to-funding data.
