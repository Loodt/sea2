## Key Decisions
Developer influencer sponsorships convert WORSE than organic content on every measurable dimension: lower conversion rates (estimated 1-2% vs 2.6% organic), higher CPA ($167-$1,500 vs $0 marginal post-compounding), and zero compounding effect. However, the comparison is category-error: sponsorships are awareness channels that seed the organic word-of-mouth flywheel, not conversion channels competing with SEO. The data gap itself is a major finding — no developer-tool company publishes sponsorship conversion data, and PostHog explicitly calls attribution 'basically impossible.' For a solo operator: invest in organic content compounding as the primary engine; sponsorships are optional, high-variance accelerators useful only when authentic creator partnerships are available.
