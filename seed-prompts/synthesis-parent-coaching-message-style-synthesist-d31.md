## Key Decisions
Stage 4 closes the remaining gap in JQ032 by adding the longitudinal engagement rule on top of the earlier mode-family and confidence-band work. The answer is not a single tone but an observation-first hybrid whose wrapper stays child-side while the ask-strength changes with obligation and engagement history. Low-confidence or new parents get more context, validation, and one small experiment; competent responsive parents get shorter, less instructional notes. Non-response does not justify blanket control in ordinary developmental coaching, but repeated missed follow-through in safety/repair/authority cases does justify bounded directive escalation.
