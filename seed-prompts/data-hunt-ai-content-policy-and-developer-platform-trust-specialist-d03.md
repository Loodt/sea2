## Key Decisions
Autonomous LinkedIn posting for developer audiences requires a five-layer guardrail architecture: (1) Legal compliance via official API + mandatory human-in-the-loop review satisfying both LinkedIn TOS and approaching EU AI Act; (2) Algorithmic evasion through breaking AI linguistic fingerprints with technical specificity, varied patterns, and authentic engagement; (3) Developer trust gates enforcing verifiable claims, named technologies, and zero generic AI filler — because developers penalize generic content, not AI assistance (Tech/AI sector AI posts actually get +7% engagement); (4) Brand safety via topic allowlists and circuit breakers at >20% engagement drops; (5) Operational limits of 1 post/day with warm-up schedules, engagement ratio monitoring, and shadowban detection. A content quality scoring rubric with 5 weighted dimensions provides the automated pre-publish gate, with composite scores routing posts to auto-approve, human review, or reject.
