## Key Decisions
Chrome beneficiation from Bushveld tailings is commercially proven at scale. Gravity spiral concentrators are the dominant technology (TRL 9, 10+ operating plants), producing metallurgical-grade concentrate at 40-42% Cr2O3 from 11-18% Cr2O3 feed at 50-65% chromium recovery. WHIMS extends recovery into the ultrafine fraction (<45 µm) and operates commercially at 7+ plants, but cannot standalone reach met-grade. The combined spiral + WHIMS approach pushes total recovery to 70-82%, exemplified by Tharisa's $55M Vulcan plant processing 340,000 t/month. Flotation achieves met-grade only at acidic pH with poor recovery (19-27%), remaining at bench scale. Enhanced gravity (Falcon) shows promise at pilot scale (42% Cr2O3, 68% recovery) but lacks Bushveld industrial deployment.
