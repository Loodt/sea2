## Key Decisions
The developer differential reaction to AI content is NOT developer-specific — it generalizes to all domain experts. Evidence from medicine (47% of physicians demand AI oversight, 89% want provenance transparency), law (600+ hallucination cases, $109K sanctions, 11 states with formal policies), and Stanford cross-disciplinary convergence all confirm a universal expertise-gap effect. What differs is the expression — developers verify through code execution, physicians through clinical oversight, lawyers through citation validation. The HERALD implication: any B2B marketing targeting domain experts (not just developers) must build proof that terminates in domain-native verification.
