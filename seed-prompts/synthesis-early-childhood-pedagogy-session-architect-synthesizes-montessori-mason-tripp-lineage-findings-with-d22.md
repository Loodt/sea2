A 3yo Jarvis session is a 5-8 minute 'wonder encounter' (not lesson) structured as a 5-beat arc: opening ritual (canvas OFF) → wonder invitation (single photo, masterly inactivity wait) → micro-story (2-3 image sequence, doctrinal weave) → proto-narration echo → closing ritual (canvas OFF). Canvas is a window that opens (4-state machine, default OFF, ~30% of session, zero text, real photos only) — in redirect sessions (the PRIMARY mode at age 3) it may be absent entirely. Four edge cases tested: silence, wants-more, dysregulation, redirect. Golden-path and redirect-variant transcripts provide minute-by-minute implementation blueprints.
