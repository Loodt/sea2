## Key Decisions
I continued the existing Q018 derivation by converting the prior ranking into an explicit trigger rule keyed to cyanide semantics at the resin feed. The resulting rule is: choose quaternary substitution alone when the high cyanide inventory is mostly complexed, upgrade to quaternary plus targeted upstream conditioning when free cyanide is genuinely high, and only reopen medium-base operation if pre-contact liquor transformation materially removes the imported high-ionic-strength/high-basicity exposure. That closes Q018 at the design-answer level: near ~57 g/L TDS, the best-supported strategy is strong-base/quaternary substitution first, with targeted cyanide-semantic conditioning only if analytics show the resin still sees a high free-cyanide burden.
