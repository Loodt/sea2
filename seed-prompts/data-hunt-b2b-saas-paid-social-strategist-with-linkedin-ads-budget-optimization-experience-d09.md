## Key Decisions
The ROI comparison of TLA vs organic-only for developer tools varies dramatically by budget tier and time horizon. Organic-only (AI-assisted, $800-$1,900/mo) delivers 388% 3-year ROI but has a 6-8 month dead zone with near-zero pipeline. The optimal hybrid strategy layers TLA onto organic once 5+ high-performing posts exist (month 2-3). Peak ROI occurs at Tier 3 ($5-10K/mo TLA + organic) with 200-350% 12-month ROI and blended CPL of $105-$165 — actually below organic-only steady-state CPL due to synergy effects. Below $3K/mo TLA, ad spend is suboptimal (campaigns never exit LinkedIn's learning phase). Above $15K/mo, diminishing returns set in for niche developer audiences. The key insight: TLA's primary ROI contribution is bridging organic's dead zone and accelerating time-to-first-deal by 4-6 months, not replacing organic's compounding returns.
