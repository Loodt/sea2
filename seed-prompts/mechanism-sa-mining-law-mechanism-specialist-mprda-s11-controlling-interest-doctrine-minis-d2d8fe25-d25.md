YES, Au-Token SPV can hold minority equity in the Retreater without triggering s11 consent, provided structure sits genuinely below Mogale's four-limb threshold (>50% share capital / voting rights / director appointment-removal power / profit-asset rights) in both form and economic substance per DMRE substance-over-form doctrine.
