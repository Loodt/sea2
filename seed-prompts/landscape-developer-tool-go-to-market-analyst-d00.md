## Key Decisions
All four marketing channels assessed with quantitative evidence. PLG has the strongest developer-specific data (OpenView: 5% free-to-paid, 20-40% activation). Content/SEO compounds but evidence is generic B2B. Paid acquisition has the worst conversion rates of any B2B channel and rising costs. Community-led growth has zero published conversion-to-revenue data. For agentic software in a trust-fragmented market, PLG + Content is the evidence-supported combination.
