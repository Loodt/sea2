## Key Decisions
Solo developer-tool marketers spend ~30-35% of time on content creation, ~20-25% on admin/low-value work, and ~15-20% on community/DevRel. Documentation and technical content are highest-leverage (confirmed by DevRel surveys, MicroConf data, and practitioner evidence). Automation ROI is highest for admin tasks, email sequences, and content repurposing — reclaiming 10-15 hrs/week. The critical insight: AI should not replace the high-leverage human-judgment work (docs, community engagement) but eliminate everything else so the solo marketer can do more of it. With AI adoption (1/3 of indie founders at >70% AI usage), the time allocation is restructuring toward community and product experience work.
