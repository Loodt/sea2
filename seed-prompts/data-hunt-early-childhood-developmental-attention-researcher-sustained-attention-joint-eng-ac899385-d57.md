## Key Decisions
JQ084 resolved: 20-min within-session is NOT precluded for age-3 Afrikaans pilot. Kill criterion (uniform <10-min sustained attention) not met in literature. Default design = single 20-min modular-within-session (4x5-min blocks + 20-30s transitions) with per-child break-trigger protocol. Split 2x10-min reserved as fallback for 2;6-3;5 band only, activated if >30% of first 6 recruits fail break-trigger recovery. Rationale stack: Kalliomaki 2025 upper-tail 25+ min under joint-engagement; Southwood 2022 30-60 min sessions work for 3;6+ Afrikaans-L1; Slining 30-42mo longitudinal shows typical task completion; split-design doubles scheduling/transport friction (the dominant attrition obstacle class). Residual risks (2;6-3;5 cohort data gap, break-trigger calibration, voice-mentor fatigue differential) parked as two follow-on questions with explicit pilot-calibration gate.
