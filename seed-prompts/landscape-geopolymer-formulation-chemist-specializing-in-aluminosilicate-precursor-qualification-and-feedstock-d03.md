## Key Decisions
Q002 is answered with quantified specifications across all four geopolymer applications. Structural grade requires Si:Al~2, >95% passing 45µm, Fe2O3<5%, and >20 MPa — Wits bulk tailings fail on all counts but slimes fraction (<63µm) blended with 10-60% fly ash/slag can reach 20-34 MPa. Refractory and fire-resistant grades require K-activation and Si:Al 1-1.9, achievable with mechanically activated slimes (91% amorphous content possible). Waste immobilisation is the lowest-barrier pathway — already TCLP compliant with >99% metal immobilization. The blocking experimental gap remains Q006: measured reactive Si:Al of the slimes fraction under alkaline activation.
