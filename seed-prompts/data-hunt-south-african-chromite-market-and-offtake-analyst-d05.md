## Key Decisions
Iteration 2 closed the main gap by separating current price bases and clarifying buyer/offtake structures. Metallurgical-grade chrome from South African tailings has a current public bulk export price anchor at US$253/t CIF China average in H1 FY2025, with spot around US$300/t by May 2025. Chemical and foundry remain live South African specialty products with active Fastmarkets FOB South Africa assessments and a clear parcel split between wet bulk industrial sales and dried-and-bagged higher-spec shipments; the latest open-web exact specialty quotes I found are September 2023 Fastmarkets assessments at US$310-330/t chemical, US$320-370/t foundry wet bulk, and US$370-415/t foundry dried-and-bagged. Refractory should not be fully killed as a product because Samancor still states it produces refractory grades, but it is materially less transparent and likely less liquid because the South African FOB refractory benchmark was discontinued on 27 December 2023 and public current prices are mostly downstream processed offer quotes rather than producer benchmarks.
