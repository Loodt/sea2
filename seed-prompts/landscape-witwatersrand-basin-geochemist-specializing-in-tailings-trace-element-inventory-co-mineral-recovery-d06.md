## Key Decisions

Uranium (~100 ppm, ~$5-9/t margin) is the only co-mineral with standalone economic viability, driven by nuclear renaissance demand and SA historical precedent, though requiring acid leach + IX circuit and NECSA regulatory compliance. All other targets — TiO2 (by-product only), PGMs (sub-economic refractory alloys), REEs (negligible), Cr/base metals (environmental parameters), vanadium (wrong geology), semiconductor metals Ge/Ga/In (wrong host mineralogy), and hydrogen economy metals (no standalone pathway) — are definitively ruled out.
