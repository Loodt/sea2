## Key Decisions
[DERIVED: synthesis] A realistic SA mineral-processing deployment pathway is a gated pilot that is treated as a controlled change under DMRE COP/MHSA-style governance, not an informal demo, with explicit authorizations, consultation, training, and auditable documentation. [DERIVED: synthesis] Integration approval is typically blocked unless radio compliance (ICASA) and OT/ICS risk-based security review inputs are ready, and gateway siting/power/data-path constraints are designed for safe-area deployment. [DERIVED: synthesis] Post-install sustainment must be framed as maintenance-of-change (inspection cadence, controlled updates, training/competency, audit trail). [SOURCE/DERIVED] Commercial rollout then depends on procurement operationalization: supplier onboarding plus platform-mediated PO/invoicing via systems like Coupa/Ariba/SAP Business Network, with contract terms (defects, inductions, service-entry timing) shaping support/SLA and RMA/spares design.
