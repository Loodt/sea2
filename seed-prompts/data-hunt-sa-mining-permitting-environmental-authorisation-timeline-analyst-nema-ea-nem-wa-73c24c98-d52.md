## Key Decisions
AUQ021 ANSWERED. The Draft MRD Bill 2025 s42A 2-year window is an APPLICATION clock, not a grant clock. Owners of historic residue stockpiles must FILE either an s102 amendment application or a fresh mining/SSMP/artisanal-permit application within 2 years of Bill promulgation; failure to apply (not failure to be granted) triggers state reversion. Under NEMA s38A One Environmental System, EA/WML/WUL RoDs gate s23 mining-right GRANT, not s22 lodgement or admission, so the 2-year clock is structurally achievable despite EA (18-24mo), WUL (6-18mo) and WML (12-24mo integrated) practical lead-times exceeding 24 months. Au-Token Phase-2 critical path: lodge integrated applications inside year 1, allow 12-month buffer for completeness defects, treat 18-36-month environmental grant-cycle as gating operational commencement (not s42A retention). KEY RISK: Bill text could change to GRANT-by during NCOP — verbatim PDF still inaccessible (secondary-source-grade evidence), monitor required.
