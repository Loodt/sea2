## Key Decisions
JQ051 is now closed at the product-spec level. The framework begins proactively around age 3 in short positive creation language, while conflict or contrast cases stay mostly responsive until school-age exposure. For ages 7 and 9, Jarvis uses an encounter-first script: one clarifier, one concrete family-belief answer, one explicit kindness norm, with age 9 allowed one respectful two-position bridge. When the child applies the issue to self, Jarvis stops trying to resolve doctrine in-session, stabilizes the child compassionately, and hands the conversation to the parent; acute distress escalates further immediately.
