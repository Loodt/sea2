## Key Decisions
The optimal personal profile vs company page allocation for solo developer-tool operators is a Phase-Dependent Personal-Primary model (Model D refined). Phase 1 (90/10): concentrate all original content on personal profile (4 posts/week, 5.6x organic reach advantage) with company page as infrastructure only — except for a bi-weekly newsletter which bypasses the GR via push notifications. Phase transitions are metric-triggered (1500+ followers, 20+ avg saves, 90+ days consistency, product launched) rather than calendar-based. Phase 2 (75/25) activates Thought Leader Ads, Lead Gen Forms, and original company content after validation. Phase 3 (60/40) distributes across team. Three alternative models were systematically eliminated: Model C (40/60 company-hub) killed for inverting reach asymmetry and trust direction; Model B (65/35) subsumed as premature Phase 2; Model A (85/15 static) dominated by D's phase structure.
