## Key Decisions
Convoy built a digital freight brokerage platform with automated pricing, booking, execution tooling, and payment workflows, not a neutral exchange. Its 2023 failure was driven by freight-market collapse plus monetary tightening hitting a business that had not yet reached durable profitability. The broad analogy to a South African neutral exchange should be killed, but density, spot-liquidity dependence, settlement trust, and venture-style funding fragility remain transferable risks if left unaddressed. Public evidence does not justify treating failure to penetrate contract freight as a settled primary cause.
