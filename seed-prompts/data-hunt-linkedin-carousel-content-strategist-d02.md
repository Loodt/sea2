## Key Decisions
QQ003 is fully answered across all four dimensions. Optimal carousels for technical audiences use 7-10 slides in portrait 1080x1350 format with 30 words max per slide, high-contrast minimalist design, and progress indicators. Structure follows hook-question opener (3x swipe-through), pattern interrupt on slide 2, one-idea-per-slide with Problem-Solution-Result arc, and specific CTA on the final slide. Topics that earn highest saves from technical professionals are frameworks/checklists, case studies with ROI metrics, data visualizations with interpretation, and architecture decision guides. Completion rate is the critical algorithm metric — front-load value before the slide 7 drop-off point, and maintain above 35% click-through to avoid visibility penalty.
