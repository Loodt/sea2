## Key Decisions
Developer traffic quality is governed by a single meta-principle: utility converts, persuasion doesn't. The highest-quality traffic comes from three sources — long-tail SEO tutorials that compound over 6-12 months (PostHog: 185K users from 100 pages), interactive documentation that functions as product trial (Stripe/Twilio model, 6x conversion over static content), and community-sourced problem-solving posts on HN/Reddit (1.5-15% conversion for engaged developers). The optimal technical product strategy is a 5-channel engine: SEO tutorials as compounding base, interactive docs as conversion mechanism, HN/Reddit for high-quality traffic spikes, email for nurture of warm prospects, and community platforms (Discord/Slack) for retention and word-of-mouth amplification. Formats that enable hands-on evaluation or solve immediate problems convert; formats that brand-build without utility (case studies, Product Hunt, paid ads) underperform by 3-10x.
