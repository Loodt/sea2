## Key Decisions
I reviewed the prior five-group core and tested the one unstable boundary left by iteration 1: whether relationship-handling notes belong in the universal brief. Using the existing adoption findings, the stricter minimum rule favors collapsing the mandatory brief to four universal groups only: transition context/current state, decision map, active advice agenda, and open loops/dated commitments. Relationship-handling context remains valuable, but only in a minority of higher-friction cases and is the field most likely to become subjective duplicate narrative work, so it should move into a tightly bounded triggered micro-annex rather than stay mandatory. Q025 is therefore answerable: the minimum justified design is a 4-field mandatory continuity brief plus a trigger-based one-prompt continuity note for exceptional first-contact risk.
